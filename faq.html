1 . what is handicrafts?

Handicrafts are handmade products created using traditional skills and techniques, often involving craftsmanship passed down through generations. These items are typically crafted using natural materials such as wood, metal, clay, fabric, and paper, and can include products like pottery, jewelry, textiles, sculptures, and home decor. Handicrafts are valued for their uniqueness, artistry, and the personal touch that goes into making each piece, often reflecting cultural heritage and creativity.
